Connect your Google Sheet

This dashboard reads and writes live data through a Google Apps Script web app. One-time setup takes about 5 minutes.

  1. Open your finance Google Sheet → Extensions → Apps Script
  2. Delete any existing code and paste the full contents of apps_script.gs
  3. Deploy → New deployment → type Web app, execute as Me, access Anyone → Deploy & authorize
  4. Copy the Web app URL and paste it below

What your HSA is worth — two engines
Your HSA pays off two ways. Engine 1 is the tax you avoid every year by paying medical bills with pre-tax, pre-FICA dollars instead of from your paycheck — certain, market-independent. Engine 2 is the small receipts you keep (don't reimburse): those dollars stay invested and compound tax-free, which is where the HSA pulls ahead of an IRA or a taxable account. Spend & hoard rates auto-fill from your ledger's trailing 12 months once it has data; override anything. Nominal dollars.
